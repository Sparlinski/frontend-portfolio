ABOUT

Hello, my name is Darren Costelloe, and I am a designer, creator, and problem solver with over 10 years of experience in the design field, primarily as an industrial designer. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working across a diverse range of industries, contributing to the design of various product forms.

As I progressed in my career, I developed a growing interest in digital design, which eventually led me to explore web development. This passion for coding and the digital space grew as I gained exposure to new disciplines and skill sets. Recognizing this shift, I decided to embrace the challenge of learning the necessary programming languages and tools to integrate this new focus into my professional journey.

Today, I am excited to embark on this next phase of my career, where I can combine my design expertise with my growing web development skills. I invite you to explore my portfolio, which showcases my work to date, and I look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you in the future.
